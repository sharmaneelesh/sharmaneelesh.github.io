About Us

Campus Binge is a young student-oriented startup that helps students making the jump to college life.

Our student fraternity is positioned as a “big brother figure” for every student moving to college.

We are a youth friendly startup that connects with students at the +2 level to guide them with college admissions and provide them with accommodation, network support and much more when they shift to college. This student-led initiative is run by our team of 50+ students and 250+ campus ambassadors spread across colleges in Delhi, Mumbai, Pune and Bangalore.

India.

The company was launched in the face of the pandemic in 2020 when market surveys and research showed great distress amongst the students regarding their college life and educational prospects.
We connect with the class school students and providing them free-of-cost admission counselling
We provide accomodation to freshers moving to college, by helping them find the correct PG/Hostel tailored to your needs
We give you the perfect college experience via our community program, helping you settle into your campuses, organising social events and also providing academic support.
