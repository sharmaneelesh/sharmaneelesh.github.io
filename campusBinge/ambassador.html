AMBASSADOR PROGRAMME

Let’s think about it. A place where students from all over India are connected in a cohesive network. Sounds confusing? Let us simplify it for you. The CampusBinge community wants to unite all the students studying in various colleges and streams all over India. those plethora of students studying in Universities from the North to South, East to West. Different regions, different courses, different streams, colleges and experiences. All brought together on a single platform. Phew, hope that cleared it up.

During the pandemic, education went online but our extracurricular activities never did. Our Programme was launched with this vision of solving that problem.
