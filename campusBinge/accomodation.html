ACCOMMODATION

We did many surveys amongst college students over the course of the past 2 years, trying to understand the problems we face. Accommodation was one of the most recurring answers. To go to an alien city and look for a PG is no joke. Brokers, middlemen and unethical mediators not only eat you through commissions but more often than misinterpret your budgeting and housing requirements.


You might even have to tread on foot through the city long hours before arriving at the correct door.Trust us, we have been there, suffered that.Thus, CampusBinge helps you find your best suited accommodation hassle free, with zero commission. Yes, you heard that right. No middlemen, no charges, no hassle, we got your back.
