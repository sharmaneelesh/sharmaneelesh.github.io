ABOUT US

Termed as 'The Wise Infant' by the circuit, Liberation is a neophyte in the world of MUNs. It was founded by a group of students just out of school, but with a purpose to revolutionize the circuit. Liberation is based on the foundations of trust, empathy and ethics, coupled with social awareness. After a successful first edition, we are back for the upgraded version, 2.0.


UN INVOLVEMENT

The Liberation MUN 2.0 has partnered with the United Nations Information Centre for India and Bhutan. We at Liberation emphasize upon the importance of International Peace and Cooperation. We have directed all our efforts, our principles for developing empathy and social awareness in the society.


A once in a lifetime experience...

The Liberation emphasizes time and again, on diversity and tolerance. Our Secretariat is dedicated to ensure the best possible experience for all delegates, without compromising with discourse. We also highlight the importance of intent, with results; we do not believe in building a manor at the expense of a thousand villages.

OUR MISSION.

Liberation, as the name suggests, defines our guiding mission. We are committed to liberating minds, liberating ideas and liberating our society. After being on the sidelines watching this discourse turn into a business, Liberation was finally founded to bring a change; because reforms correct mistakes, but initiatives correct conscience.
